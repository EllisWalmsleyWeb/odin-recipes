Steak and Eggs

Description

Steak and eggs is a dish of beefsteak and fried eggs. Originating in Australian cuisine, it is most typically served as a breakfast or brunch food.

  • Thin steak
  • Kosher salt and black pepper
  • Eggs
  • Garnish of choice
  1. Start by seasoning the steak and grilling it. Generally speaking, you should grill it for about 2 minutes per side for medium-rare, or a total of 3 minutes in a dual-contact grill. If you're using a strip steak, you should also briefly cook the fat strip.
  2. If you're pan-frying the steak and are aiming for medium-rare, you'll need to cook it for 2-3 minutes per side over medium-high heat.
  3. Fry the eggs. While the meat is resting, quickly fry the eggs in butter or avocado oil.
  4. Season and serve. Season the dish with salt, black pepper, and your chosen garnish, then serve.
